Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Our Commitment to Privacy

Creative Coloring is a coloring game for young children. We do not require account signup and we are committed to protecting your child's privacy by minimizing data collection.

Information We Handle

  • Local data: game activity is stored temporarily in your browser’s localStorage to track recent sessions. This data is never sent to our servers and is not personally identifiable.
  • Contact messages: if you (the parent) choose to contact us via our form, we receive the information you submit to respond to your inquiry.

Cookies and Ads

We do not use cookies. The entire game is ad-free and self-contained in your browser to ensure a safe experience for your child.

Your Choices

  • You can clear your browser's site data at any time to remove any locally stored game activity.
  • Using your browser's private or incognito mode will prevent any session data from being stored.

Contact

Questions? Parents can use the contact form on the Home page.
