FAQ

Answers to common questions about learning with Creative Coloring.

Is QuizMania a safe environment?

Absolutely. The game is designed to be a safe space for everyone. It contains no intrusive ads or in-app purchases, allowing you to focus on the quiz.

Can I save my score?

Yes! Your scores are saved locally in your browser so you can track your progress on the leaderboard.

Can I play on a tablet or phone?

Yes. The game is fully responsive and designed to work perfectly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

How do I play?

The controls are simple: read the question and tap the correct answer. It's designed to be intuitive for everyone.

Is this game free?

Absolutely. QuizMania is completely free. We are committed to providing a fun experience for everyone without any cost.
